About Gabion Walls

Gabion walls are wire mesh containers filled with stones, rocks, or other durable materials. They are widely used in civil engineering for erosion control, slope stabilization, and coastal protection. Our gabion wall construction at Beruwala/Earl's Reef demonstrates our expertise in creating robust, environmentally-friendly solutions for coastal protection.

Project Details

High-Quality Materials

We use premium-grade galvanized steel wire mesh and carefully selected stones for maximum durability and longevity.

Expert Engineering

Our team of experienced engineers ensures proper design and installation for optimal performance in coastal conditions.

Environmental Considerations

Gabion walls allow natural drainage and vegetation growth, making them an eco-friendly solution for coastal protection.

Durability & Longevity

Properly constructed gabion walls can last for decades, providing long-term protection against erosion and wave action.

Cost-Effective Solution

Gabion walls offer an economical alternative to concrete structures while maintaining excellent performance.

Flexibility

The flexible nature of gabion structures allows them to adapt to ground movement and settling.
