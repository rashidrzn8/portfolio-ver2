about us

PNConstructions: Turning your dream buildings into reality!

At PNConstructions, we specialize in construction, architectural design, quantity surveying, and project management across Sri Lanka. From luxury villas and hotels to steel structures and gabion walls, we ensure every project is completed with precision, quality, and safety. Our expert team supports you from concept to completion.

Our Highlight: We are experts in Gabion Wall & Revetment construction, with extensive experience in coastal protection projects including our notable work at Beruwala / Earl's Reef. Our gabion wall solutions provide durable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective protection against erosion and wave action.
